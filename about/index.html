About Us

The Aramean Center is a public hub that serves as a free comprehensive archive of knowledge, resources, and materials that revolve around the Aramean nation.

History

Aramean Center was developed as a project by The Aramean Institute in late 2021.

Mission

Our goal is to gather as much information as possible about the entire Aramean heritage with the aim of sharing it with the public. We aim to preserve and enrich the Aramean heritage through the collection and sharing of data and information gathered from the public.
