About Us

The Aramean Center is a public hub that serves as a free comprehensive archive of knowledge, resources, and materials that revolve around the Aramean nation.

History

Aramean Center was established by Aramean Institute in late 2021 with the aim of preserving and enriching the Aramean heritage through gathering, storing, sharing reliable data and statistics.

Mission

Our goal is to gather as much information as possible about the entire Aramean heritage with the aim of sharing it.
