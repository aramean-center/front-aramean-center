About Us

Aramean Center is a public digital archive dedicated to collecting, preserving, and sharing knowledge related to the Aramean people, heritage, history, language, and culture. It is a free and accessible platform designed to organize information and resources in one centralized place for public use.

The platform also enables community participation through contributions, registrations, and the submission of missing or updated data, helping to build a continuously growing and more complete archive.

History

Aramean Center was developed in 2021 by Aramean Institute as a public-facing archive platform focused on making Aramean-related information more accessible and organized.

The project was created to address the fragmentation of historical and cultural materials across different platforms and collections. Aramean Center brings this information together into a structured digital archive for public access and long-term preservation.

Mission

The mission of Aramean Center is to preserve and make Aramean heritage accessible by providing an open digital archive for public use.

The platform focuses on organizing and sharing information related to Aramean history, language, literature, traditions, religion, historical geography, media, and community records.

Aramean Center also encourages public participation by allowing individuals to contribute data, submit corrections, and help expand the archive, ensuring continuous growth and improved accuracy over time.
